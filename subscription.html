Be in charge of your own success
Nowadays, making a living out of your art and building a successful career doesn't have to depend on finding an art gallery or on conforming with what museums or art critics think art should be.
Instead, you can reclaim your career by building an audience as large and diverse as possible to make sure your art is seen by those who will appreciate it.
Moreover, as you gain a larger audience, the balance of power is reversed in your favour and it's the traditional art world which has to engage with you.
It used to be a time were you couldn't easily show your work directly to art lovers out there. Museums, newspapers and galleries were the only communication channels for artists.
Now, there are very little barriers, except those others put in your mind, to reach out to like-minded people. Open Instagram or Twitter, and you'll find people like you and me with thousands of engaged followers.
As a team of tech-savvy young art professionals, who have worked in blue-chip and experimental art galleries in Europe and the United Kindown, we are convinced that, with the right knowledge and the right tools, all artists can build successful careers on their own.
That's why we have put all our efforts and knowledge in the Artist Business Tool Subscription to empower you to succeed.
Join now to be in charge of your own success!!
Embrace the 21st century
Every week, you will receive materials to learn how to leverage modern digital techniques to build your own audience and sell directly to its members.
We'll show you the key ingredients of a successful digital communication and content strategy which makes use of the full breadth of the social media landscape, with Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin and many more. We'll also cover you can boost your reach and sales with programmatic ads.
At the same time, we think that a successful career can benefit from more traditional methods from the art world, such as contacting art advisors, established collectors, designers, and curators.
We'll share with you how you can use these techniques to your advantage without the need of middlemen such as art galleries. Every week, you'll receive a list of people who could be interested in your work, along with advice on how to approach them.

Stay revelant
What the best digital communication and sales techniques are changes very rapidly. What used to be hard to do becomes a must do. The social media landscape can be entirely disrupted in a few months' time, with platforms like TikTok becoming global phenomenon.
Morevoer, staying up to date with the latest news from the art world is extremely important to know what art buyers are looking for.
We leverage our experience and comb the internet to inform you of these trends. As part of the subscription, you'll receive a weekly summary of the upcoming must-see exhibitions openings, and the must-read think pieces on the art market and on digital communication.
