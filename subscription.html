Be in charge of your own success
In 2020, artists can have successful careers and support themselves financially without art galleries.
The global pandemic has accelerated the shift online. Initiatives like the Artist Support Pledge on Instagram have helped many artists realise the power of reaching out directly to art-lovers.
By building an online audience of people who identify with your art and engaging effectively with it, you can sell much more than you would with traditional techniques.
Many artists have already done so, why not you?
Join the Artist Business Tool Subscription now to receive each week in-depth content, tools, challenges, and dedicated support to help you grow your online sales.
Who is behind the Artist Business Tool Subscription?
After working 5 years in art galleries in France, the Netherlands and the United Kingdom, I've met many artists from all backgrounds and practices. I've learnt about their careers, their financial struggles, and how broken the gallery system is for them.
I've also studied in-depth successful creators such as musicians, and direct-to-consumer brands, to understand how they were using social media and new technology to build their online audience and sell to its members directly.
With the Artist Business Tool Subscription, I've decided to combine my experience of the art world and my knowledge of online sales best practices.
It's a program that's meant to bring you from not knowing anything about online sales, to being able to support yourself financially entirely with your art.
