About
Gavriella Abekassis is the founder of Out of the Cube, which helps artists manage their careers and sell their artworks. Out of the Cube offers Artist Business Tools, such as articles published regularly, and consultancy services.
Gavriella Abekassis has worked in art galleries in London, Amsterdam, and Paris. She co-founded perform doc (Amsterdam), a project of performance documentation, and created Art must be Tasty (London), commissioning performance Art.
She co-wrote a book on the Art Deco designer René Prou published by Editions Norma (Paris) in 2017, after a Master of Research in Art History at the Sorbonne, Paris.
