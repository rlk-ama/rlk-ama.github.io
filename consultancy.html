How does Out of the Cube help artists?
On the one hand, creators such as musicians have found ways to leverage modern digital techniques to grow their audience and have successful careers, which, as a millennial and a heavy user of platforms such as Instagram and Youtube, I relate to very much.
On the other hand, after 5 years working in traditional art galleries in France, the Netherlands and the United Kingdom, I grew frustrated with how little time we would dedicate to the artists we represented.
If a gallery has more than 12 artists, which is actually a low number, and if solo exhibitions are a month long, that means that each artist has the full attention of his or her gallery only once a year!
That's why I decided that I could combine both my work experience in galleries and my digital know-how to offer artists all the attention they deserve and give them a better chance at making a living out of their art.

Sales growth and career strategy
As an artist, you don't necessarily have the time, the skills and the energy to focus on the more commercial aspects of your career.
What you are good at, and what your should dedicate yourself to, is working on your art pratice.
However, without financial stability and enough runway, the financial aspects of your life will start eating up more and more of your energy and quickly become a source of intense anxiety.
We can assist you with our experience to:
  • Establish medium and long term business plans to grow your career: having a clear vision with concrete milestones along the way is the only way to have your career move forward.
  • Develop your own audience online and grow your direct to consumer sales: we have an extensive experience with best practices and ads on platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, Youtube, Patreon, etc.
  • Grow your sales using traditional techniques: we identify together the best opportunities for you among our portfolio of traditional art professionals, such as art advisors, collectors and curators.
  • Get an external opinion on your website, portfolio, finances, social media strategy, etc. from a professional who has worked with artists from all backgrounds and artistic practices.

Kickstarting your career as a recent art school graduate
The art world is a daunting place and art schools usually don't prepare you well to what comes after. After your graduation show, you're left on your own to figure out how to set up your company, pay your taxes, find a gallery, develop your audience, etc.
We will:
  • Introduce you the art world and its professionals: what are art advisors, how to contact curators, how do galleries operate, etc.
  • Give you the personal finance and business lectures art schools didn't: what are the different types of company you can set up to start selling your artworks, how do taxes and VAT work, etc.
  • Establish a plan to build your audience and grow your career: design a social media strategy, introduce you to their advertisement platforms, help you set up a mailing list and a website, work on your medium and long term career plan.
