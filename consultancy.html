How can you sell your artworks online?

Why Out of the Cube?
After 5 years working in traditional art galleries in France, the Netherlands and the United Kingdom, I realised two things.
First, galleries dedicate very little time to the artists they represent, and are stuck in the past when it comes to selling artworks online.
Second, many of the artists I've met over the years were disillusioned about galleries and were very curious about building independent careers.
My passion has always been working with artists. Therefore, I decided to start Out of the Cube to help you take your career into your own hands.
